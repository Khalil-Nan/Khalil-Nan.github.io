# 3 Interpreting and Asking Questions about Time-Frequency Results

## 3.1 EEG Time-Frequency: The Basics

<mark>power  is the amount of energy in a frequency band and is the squared amplitude of the oscillation.</mark>power和phase是独立的

三方面:

1. 频率

2. 相位

3. power
- 关于EEG到底测量的是什么东西:The oscillations measured by EEG reflect fluctuations in dendritic electrical activity of  populations of neurons.

- 时间和频率的precision是互为成本的

- background activity就是不受task events调节，而且很难用神经认知过程解释，因此baseline normalization很有用

- 对于task-related activity可以分为phase-locked和non-phase-locked两类，<mark>有没有比较好的例子可以举例的</mark>

- <mark>and all ERPs are examples  of phase-locked activities.</mark>

- 有一个电极记录不同的neural source的oscillation，也有不同的电极记录同一个neural source的问题，这个问题影响连接性分析

## 3.2 Ways to View Time-Frequency Results

1. the frequency slice横轴是频率，纵轴是power

2. time slice，横轴是时间纵轴是power,意思就是针对某一特定频率

3. space slice ，就是对某一频率<mark>or the average over multiple adjacent time-frequency points,</mark>某一时间，我猜指标是power，这样才有利于topographical localization

4. time-frequency slice
