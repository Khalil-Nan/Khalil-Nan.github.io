## 地道用法

Timing is crucial, though.

Some sleep experts contend that humans **are biologically disposed to **nap.

Many people recognise the feeling of post-lunch lethargy; few **give into it**.

Sleep scientists tend to agree that a short “power” nap, somewhere between ten and 30 minutes, is best.
